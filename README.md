# JourneyOne

## One App. Every Journey.

JourneyOne is an AI-powered mobility platform developed by Great Creations that helps users travel seamlessly using buses, metro, trains, autos, bike taxis, cabs, and walking routes while prioritizing safety, affordability, and convenience.

---

## Vision

To become India's most trusted mobility and safety platform by providing intelligent transportation guidance, real-time travel assistance, and family safety features in a single application.

---

## Problem Statement

Daily commuters often face:

- Difficulty finding the best route
- Lack of real-time transportation information
- Multiple apps for different transport services
- Last-mile transportation challenges
- Safety concerns during travel
- Difficulty tracking family members during journeys
- Lost belongings during transit

JourneyOne aims to solve these problems through a unified AI-powered platform.
